A decentralized platform to trade Digital Assets with Real Assets

EthPayCash aims to provide a platform where
users can easily exchange their Digital/Virtual Assets with Real World Assets.

What is EthPayCash ?

EthPayCash is a first of its kind blockchain platform that facilitates a decentralised exchange of virtual goods/services for their real world counterparts.

Technology Involved in EthPayCash ?

EthPayCash uses the power of Smart Contracts deployed on the Ethereum Blockchain. Thus making the contract completely decentralized and secure.
